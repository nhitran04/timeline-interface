Putting Goals and Actions Together!

Now that you know how to give instructions to the robot in terms of goals and actions, you are now free to instruct the robot however you would like to!

  • If you give instructions in terms of actions, you are telling the robot what it needs to do. The robot will fill in any missing steps needed to achieve an action.
  • If you give instructions in terms of goals, you are telling the robot what it needs to achieve. The robot will plan the sequence of actions to achieve a goal.
  • You may provide the robot with instructions in the form of goals, actions, or a combination of both!
